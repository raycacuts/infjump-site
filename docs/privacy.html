Privacy Policy — InfJump

Effective date: 2025-08-31

Overview

InfJump does not collect personal data from players. The game runs offline and does not require an account.

Game Center

InfJump optionally uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Any profile, friends, or scores shown in Game Center are processed by Apple under Apple’s policies. You can sign out of Game Center in iOS Settings if you prefer not to use it.

Data We Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact info
  • No precise location
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking
